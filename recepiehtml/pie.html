Grandmas's Lemon Merengue Pie

One 9-inch pie
30 min-prep time
10 Min-cook time
40 minute total
8 servings

Ingridients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups of water
  • 2 lemons, juiced, zested
  • 2 tbsp of butter
  • 4 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6 tbsp white sugar

Directions

  • 1. preheat oven: preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees celsius)
  • 2. make lemon filling: in a medium saucepan
