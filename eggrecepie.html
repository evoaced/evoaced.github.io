Scrambled Eggs

eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

method

  1. melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. once butter is melted add cream and the eggs
  4. using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds(as if you were making an omelet)
  5. when the eggs are still moist remove them from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
